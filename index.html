London, UK

My dad was in the air force for 20 years, he was stationed in Saudia Arabia, my mom was at a military base in England when I was born. I was actually born in an ambulance on the way to the hospital, and have dual citizenship. We lived in England for about a year before they moved back to the US and lived in Texas.

Wichita Falls, TX

After we moved back to the states, we moved to Texas and lived there for about 5 years, I don't have many memories of that time. I just know we lived directly across the street from my elementary school, and my friend Nick lived right up the street.

Foley, MO

We decided to move back up to Missouri when I was five to be closer to our family. My uncle was a real estate agent at the time and found us a house out in the middle of nowhere, they still live at that house. I moved away from there when i was 21 years old.

Wentzville, MO

I eventually rented an apartment at Peine Lakes apartments where I lived for a couple of years. I loved living in Wentzville and being so close to everything for the first time in my life. When I lived in Foley it would usually take 15 minutes or more to get to any type of store.

Lake St. Louis, MO

In 2015 I rented out my best friends basement, where I got to spend a lot of time with him and his family. I got to learn a lot about what a Godly family looks like from him, his wife, and their two boys. I learned a lot about raising children, before I moved in with them I had never even changed a diaper. Also while living with them I met my future wife to be at our church. We eventually got engaged and married, where I then moved into my wifes house in O'fallon.

O'Fallon, MO

My wife, Kayla and I, got married last year in May. She had bought this home a couple years previously so it made it easy to already have a place to move into. It's a cozy little home, we have two dogs and wish we had a big fenced in back yard. For right now though this home will do until we decide to start having children.
